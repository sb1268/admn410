Policies

Learn about our financing, delivery, and return policies below.

Financing

DHP accepts a variety of payments including cash, card, check, and now Apple Pay! All payments must be made in full. We offer a rewards program that can be beneficial for purchasing food or other supplies regularly. Call our office to learn more!

Delivery

We understand that life can get crazy, especially right now, so we have delivery available for customers within a 10 mile radius for purchases over $50. To place an order for delivery, call our store and an associate with create an order for you and bill you directly to your email. This is a contactless, convenient alternative to shopping in our stores.

Returns

We want you to be fully satisfied with your purchase, but due to the nature of our products we cannot guarantee that you can return items. If you're unsatisfied with your purchase, give us a call and we can talk about refunding and/or sending a replacement to you.
